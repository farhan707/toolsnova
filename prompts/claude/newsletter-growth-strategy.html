📋 Prompt
You are a newsletter growth strategist who has helped creators build 10,000+ subscriber lists.

Topic: [newsletter topic]
Target reader: [who it's for — job title, interest, problem it solves]
Current subscribers: [count or 'starting from zero']
Frequency: [weekly/bi-weekly/daily]
Platform: [Beehiiv/Substack/ConvertKit/Mailchimp/Ghost]

Task:
1. POSITIONING: Specific transformation delivered, how you differ from 10 other newsletters on this topic, tagline (under 10 words)

2. CONTENT FORMAT: Issue structure, ideal length/reading time, content ratio (original insight/curation/product)

3. ACQUISITION (5 channels): Tactic, effort level, expected monthly gains at your current size

4. REFERRAL PROGRAMME: Structure, incentives, how to promote within the newsletter

5. VIRAL ISSUE FORMATS: 3 issue types most likely to be forwarded

6. MONETISATION PATH: 0 → 1K → 5K → 10K — what revenue options open at each

7. 90-DAY PLAN: Week-by-week targets and actions
📄 Example output
NEWSLETTER STRATEGY: AI Tools for Marketers

POSITIONING:
Transformation: From inconsistent AI output to reliable professional results every time.
Differentiator: We test every prompt on real client work before sharing — not theoretical.
Tagline: 'Tested AI prompts for marketing professionals'

CONTENT FORMAT (5-minute read):
• THE PROMPT OF THE WEEK: One tested prompt with example output (3 min)
• THE TIP: One observation from AI tool use this week (1 min)
• THE TOOL: One discovery or comparison (1 min)

ACQUISITION:
1. Twitter/X threads (3×/week): One free prompt → 'Full version + 3 more in this week's issue' → +50–100/month
2. LinkedIn article: 'AI prompt templates I use for every content type' → +30–80/month
3. SEO landing page targeting 'free AI marketing prompts newsletter' → +20–40/month (compounds)
4. Newsletter cross-promos with 3 complementary newsletters → +100–200/month
5. Product Hunt newsletter launch → +50–200/month (one-time spike)

MONETISATION:
0–1K: Build and don't monetise — focus on making it unmissable
1K–5K: Affiliate links for tools you genuinely recommend → £500–£2K/month
5K–10K: Sponsorships from AI tool companies → £500–£1,500/issue
10K+: Paid tier, cohort course, or consulting pipeline

90-DAY PLAN:
Week 1–2: Set up Beehiiv, write 3 archive issues, build landing page
Week 3–8: Publish weekly, 3 Twitter threads/week, 1 LinkedIn article
Week 9–12: First cross-promo swaps, submit to newsletter directories

💡 Pro Tips
Most shareable newsletters have one idea done brilliantly, not five done adequately
Your referral programme works best when the reward is more of what subscribers already value — more prompts, early access
First 100 subscribers are hardest — email your existing network personally, not by broadcasting
Forward rate is the real signal at small scale — 'who shared this?' matters more than open rate
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Too comprehensive — narrower topics with high relevance outperform broad topics with lower engagement
No consistent format — readers subscribe for a reliable experience; changing the format breaks the habit
Too much curation, too little original insight — curation is easy; original perspective is why they subscribed to you
Not emailing when busy — 2-week gaps kill momentum and open rates
❓ FAQ
  • Beehiiv vs Substack vs ConvertKit?
    Beehiiv: better analytics, referral programme built in, more growth features. Substack: built-in discovery, easy paid subscriptions, less customisation. ConvertKit: best for automations and selling products. For newsletter-first growth, Beehiiv is currently strongest.
  • How often to send?
    Weekly is the gold standard — frequent enough to build habit, infrequent enough to maintain quality. Consistency matters more than frequency.
  • Target open rate?
    Industry average 20–25%. Good newsletter: 35–45%. Excellent: 50%+. Guard against list decay by sending consistently.
  • Best model for newsletter strategy?
    Claude — realistic growth projections, understands content economics, generates specific actionable tactics rather than generic advice.
