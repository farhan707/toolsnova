💡 Pro Tips
Every platform has a different audience contract — LinkedIn is professional value, TikTok is utility or entertainment, Twitter is conversation
Never post the same caption across all platforms — voice, length, and link strategy all differ
First sentence of every caption is most important — it appears in preview and determines whether the rest gets read
Save best-performing captions as templates — if a structure works once, it will likely work again
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Hashtag stuffing on LinkedIn — 3-5 relevant hashtags; 20 looks like spam
Links in Instagram post body — not clickable; always put in bio and reference in caption
Same length everywhere — Twitter must be short, Facebook can be longer, LinkedIn medium, TikTok very short
Generic CTA — specific CTAs consistently outperform 'check the link in bio'
❓ FAQ
  • How many hashtags?
    Instagram: 5-10. LinkedIn: 3-5. Twitter/X: 0-2 (hashtags reduce engagement on X; use only for events or trending topics). TikTok: 3-5. Facebook: 0-3.
  • Same posting time on all platforms?
    Convenient but not optimal. Check each platform's analytics for when your audience is active — varies significantly by niche. Scheduling tools like Buffer or Later help.
  • ChatGPT or Claude?
    ChatGPT for short punchy captions matching social media energy. Claude for LinkedIn posts requiring sustained professional argument. ChatGPT's natural brevity wins for Instagram/TikTok/Twitter.
  • Engagement rate vs follower count?
    Engagement rate matters more for monetisation. 1,000 highly engaged followers convert at higher rates than 10,000 passive ones. Focus on saves and shares — the highest-value actions on most platforms.
