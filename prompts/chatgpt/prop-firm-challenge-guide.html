📋 Prompt
You are a funded trader and coach who has passed 15+ prop firm challenges and helped 200+ traders through theirs.

DISCLAIMER: Trading involves significant risk. Prop firm rules vary — always verify current rules directly with your firm.

Firm/Challenge: [firm name or type — FTMO/Funding Pips/MyFundedFX/other]
Account size: [$10K/$25K/$50K/$100K]
Profit target: [% — typically 8-10%]
Max daily loss: [% — typically 4-5%]
Max total drawdown: [% — typically 10%]
Primary instrument: [XAU/USD/EUR/USD/NAS100/other]
Typical win rate: [your win rate]
Typical R:R: [your risk:reward ratio]

Task:
1. THE NUMBERS: Dollar limits per day and total; required daily profit to hit target
2. LOT SIZE CALCULATION: Max lots at 1% risk, 0.5% risk, with different SL sizes; how to adjust in drawdown
3. PACING STRATEGY: Optimal pace without rushing; when to reduce risk near target
4. RULE VIOLATION PREVENTION: Top 3 ways traders fail challenges and how to avoid each
5. WEEKLY MILESTONES: Week-by-week targets for a 30-day challenge
6. RECOVERY PLAN: If you lose 2% on day 1, exactly what to do next
📄 Example output
CHALLENGE STRATEGY: $25K FTMO-Style
DISCLAIMER: Verify all rules with your firm.

THE NUMBERS:
Account: $25,000 | Target: $2,000 (8%)
Firm daily limit: $1,000 | Personal daily stop: $800 (20% buffer for spread/slippage)
Total drawdown: $2,500 — treat $2,000 as your real floor

LOT SIZE (XAU/USD):
1% risk ($250) with 50-pip SL → 0.50 lots
1% risk with 100-pip SL → 0.25 lots
0.5% risk: halve both

DRAWDOWN ADJUSTMENT:
Down 2% from peak → reduce lots by 50%
Down 4% from peak → stop trading for the day
Never average down on a losing trade

PACING (30 days):
Week 1: $400 (slow start, learn the market)
Week 2: $600 | Week 3: $600 | Week 4: $400 (reduce risk, protect gains)
Total: $2,000 with buffer

COMMON FAILURES:
1. Revenge trading → mandatory 15-min break after any losing trade
2. Weekend gap risk → close ALL trades before Friday close
3. News trading without checking rules → many firms prohibit holding through NFP/FOMC

RECOVERY AFTER -2% DAY:
1. Stop trading immediately for rest of that day
2. Next day: risk only 0.25% per trade
3. A+ setups only — quality over quantity
4. Return to 1% only after 2 consecutive profitable days

💡 Pro Tips
Your personal daily stop should be 20% below the firm's limit — buffer for spread, slippage, commissions
Track equity high-water mark daily — drawdowns are often measured from the peak, not the starting balance
Week 4 is where most challenges fail — reduce risk near the target, don't increase it
Never trade the news release itself — spreads widen dramatically and stops get hit before the intended move
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Full lot size from day 1 — start at 50% until you have 5 consecutive profitable days
No personal daily stop below the firm's limit — one bad session can end the challenge
Focusing on the profit target instead of rule compliance — compliance is the only real focus
Not accounting for swap costs — overnight swaps can erode small profits significantly on XAU/USD
❓ FAQ
  • Which prop firm to start with?
    FTMO (well-established, clear rules) or Funding Pips are common starting points. Check current terms directly — this space changes rapidly.
  • Win rate needed to pass?
    With 1:2 R:R, you break even at 34% win rate. For a comfortable pass: 45%+ win rate with 1:2 R:R gives strong positive expectancy.
  • Can I use an EA?
    Rules vary by firm. Some allow EAs, some prohibit them. Always verify directly with the firm before using any automated system.
  • Best model?
    Claude maintains mathematical consistency across a multi-week plan and builds in the psychological safeguards most traders need.
