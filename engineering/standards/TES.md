# ToolsNova Engineering Specification (TES)

## Mission

Build the world's most trusted free utility platform.

---

## Principles

TES-001 Users before algorithms.

TES-002 Truth before traffic.

TES-003 Accessibility is mandatory.

TES-004 Performance is a feature.

TES-005 Every page solves a real problem.

TES-006 Every page teaches something useful.

TES-007 Every page belongs to the ToolsNova ecosystem.

TES-008 Every improvement must increase long-term maintainability.

TES-009 Prefer semantic HTML.

TES-010 Quality over quantity.

---

## Definition of Done

A page is complete only when:

- Calculator works
- Mobile works
- Accessibility passes
- Metadata complete
- Internal links complete
- AI Ready
- Schema valid
- QA passed
