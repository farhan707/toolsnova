📋 Prompt
You are a Twitter/X growth strategist who has grown accounts from zero to 50,000+ followers in focused niches.

Niche: [your niche or expertise]
Goal: [followers/newsletter growth/product sales/professional reputation]
Current followers: [count or 'starting from zero']
Posting capacity: [realistic posts per day you can sustain]

Task:
1. POSITIONING: Your unique angle — why follow you and not the 10 other accounts in your niche
2. CONTENT PILLARS (4): Each — purpose, format, frequency, 5 specific example tweets
3. TWEET FORMATS: Hook tweet, Thread (5-10 tweets), Opinion take, Engagement post
4. THREAD TEMPLATE: Fill-in-the-blank structure for your best-performing thread type
5. GROWTH TACTICS (3 specific): Each — tactic, time per day, expected result
6. WHAT NOT TO DO: 5 mistakes that kill accounts in your niche
7. 90-DAY FOLLOWER PLAN
📄 Example output
TWITTER/X STRATEGY: AI Tools for Professionals

POSITIONING: 'I test AI tools on real professional work — telling you exactly what worked, what didn't, which prompt made the difference. No hype. Just results.'
Unique angle: practitioner specificity. Not 'AI is amazing' — 'this specific prompt on Claude produced this specific output'.

PILLARS:
1. PRACTICAL TIPS (daily): 'Stop asking AI to write your email. Paste your bullet points and say: convert these into a professional email in my voice [paste 2 examples]. 10× better output.'
2. THREADS (3×/week): 'I tested 7 ways to use Claude for content writing. Here's what actually worked:'
3. OPINION TAKES (2×/week): 'The problem with AI content isn't the AI. Most people use it to skip thinking rather than think better.'
4. ENGAGEMENT (1×/week): 'What's the AI task you've tried and couldn't quite get right? Drop it below.'

THREAD TEMPLATE:
Tweet 1 (HOOK): 'I [action] for [time]. Here's what I learned: 🧵'
Tweets 3-8 (POINTS): 'Point N: [specific insight] — Exactly how: [example or prompt]'
Tweet 10 (CTA): 'If useful, [follow/RT]. I post [cadence] on [topic].'

90 DAYS:
Day 30: 100 followers — 3 tweets/day + 5 quality replies/day to big accounts in niche
Day 60: 300 followers — first thread semi-viral
Day 90: 600+ followers — first cross-promotion with newsletter

💡 Pro Tips
Replies to larger accounts consistently outperform tweets for follower acquisition — reply to the 20 biggest accounts in your space daily
First tweet in a thread is the only one most people read — make it a standalone value statement
Specificity beats generalisation — '7 AI prompts for marketers' beats 'AI is transforming marketing'
Post at consistent times — the algorithm rewards predictable cadence
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Posting without engaging — 50% of growth comes from replies, not publishing
Threads without a strong hook tweet — if tweet 1 doesn't stop the scroll, nobody reads tweet 2
Inconsistent voice — mixing professional insights with unrelated personal content confuses your audience
No conversion path — growing followers without moving them to newsletter or product is building on rented land
❓ FAQ
  • How many tweets per day?
    3-5 per day is the sweet spot. Under 2 is too slow. Over 7, quality usually suffers. Consistency over 90 days matters more than any single tweet.
  • Best time to post?
    Global: 7-9am EST and 12-2pm EST. UK-focused: 7-9am GMT. Check your Twitter Analytics for when your specific audience is active.
  • Hashtags on X?
    Low value in 2026. 1-2 for industry topics only. Avoid hashtag stacking — it looks spammy and reduces engagement.
  • ChatGPT or Claude?
    ChatGPT is stronger — its natural punchiness matches the format. Claude is better for threads requiring nuanced sustained argument across 10 tweets.
