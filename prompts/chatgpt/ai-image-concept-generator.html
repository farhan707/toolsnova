📋 Prompt
You are a creative director with 20 years of experience directing photography and AI image generation.

Brief: [what this image is for and what it must communicate]
Usage: [website hero/social media/print/presentation]
Style preference: [or 'open']
Brand/tone: [premium/playful/minimal/bold/technical]

Generate 5 distinct concepts:

For EACH:
CONCEPT NAME: [evocative title]
DIRECTION: [2–3 sentences — emotional and visual communication]
COMPOSITION: [specific composition]
COLOUR PALETTE: [3–5 colours with hex codes]
LIGHTING: [description]
REFERENCES: [2 references — photographer, artist, or film]
FULL AI PROMPT: [ready to paste into Midjourney v6 or DALL-E 3]
--no [negative elements]

FINAL: Recommended concept for [usage] + why + first to test

People convert 30–40% better than abstract for B2B tool hero images

Generate 20+ variations before selecting — AI image requires iteration

⚠️ Common Mistakes
Brief too vague — specificity is everything with AI image tools
No iteration — the first output is rarely the best
Forgetting the negative prompt — telling AI what NOT to include is as important as what to include
No colour specification — AI makes choices that may conflict with your brand
❓ FAQ
  • Best AI image tool for website heroes?
    Midjourney v6 for aesthetically sophisticated images. DALL-E 3 for literal execution. Flux.1 Dev for precise compositional control. Verify commercial use terms for your plan.
  • Image size for website hero?
    Desktop: 1440×810px minimum (16:9). Mobile: 375×500px (4:5). Generate at full resolution, crop as needed.
  • AI images legal for commercial use?
    Midjourney (paid), DALL-E 3 (Plus): commercial use permitted. Flux.1 Schnell: non-commercial. Adobe Firefly: IP-indemnified. Always verify current ToS for your specific plan.
  • Claude vs ChatGPT?
    Claude produces more original visual territories and stronger emotional reasoning. ChatGPT is faster at generating many prompt variations within a territory.
