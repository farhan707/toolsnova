📋 Prompt
You are a YouTube strategist who has grown channels from zero to 100,000+ subscribers.

Channel topic: [channel topic]
Target audience: [target audience]
Content goal: [educate/entertain/sell/mix]
Current state: [new channel or existing with X subscribers]

Task: Build a complete YouTube channel strategy:

1. NICHE POSITIONING:
Specific sub-niche you own, who you're NOT making content for, 10-word value proposition

2. CONTENT PILLARS (3–4): For each — name, 5 video ideas, primary keyword, recommended format

3. VIDEO SEO: Title formula, description template, thumbnail principles

4. UPLOAD SCHEDULE: Realistic frequency, Shorts strategy alongside long-form

5. FIRST 10 VIDEOS: Specific titles in recommended publish order with reasoning

6. MONETISATION PATH: 0 → 1K subscribers → 4K watch hours → AdSense → beyond

7. 90-DAY MILESTONES: Specific measurable targets for days 30, 60, and 90
📄 Example output
YOUTUBE STRATEGY: AI Tools for Creators

NICHE: 'AI productivity for one-person creative businesses'
Not for: Enterprise teams or people wanting AI to replace creativity.
Value proposition: 'AI tools that actually save creators time — tested, not theoretical.'

PILLARS:
1. TOOL TUTORIALS (40%) — screen recording 8–15 min — 'How to Use Claude to Edit YouTube Scripts'
2. WORKFLOW SYSTEMS (30%) — 'My Complete AI Content System: 0 to Published in 2 Hours'
3. COMPARISONS (20%) — 'ChatGPT vs Claude for Content Writing — 30-Day Honest Test'
4. SHORTS (10%) — single tips clipped from long-form

FIRST 10 VIDEOS (in order):
1. 'I Tested 10 AI Writing Tools So You Don't Have To' — establishes credibility
2. 'The ChatGPT Prompt That Writes My Newsletter in 20 Minutes' — high search volume
3. 'Stop Using AI Wrong: One Change That Doubled My Output Quality'
4. 'Claude vs ChatGPT for Freelance Writers — 30-Day Test'
5. 'My AI Workflow: 5 Content Pieces Per Week in 4 Hours'

90-DAY MILESTONES:
Day 30: 5 videos, 50 subscribers, first comment engagement
Day 60: 10 videos, 200 subscribers, 1 Short with 1K+ views
Day 90: 15 videos, 500 subscribers, 1 video with 500+ organic views

💡 Pro Tips
Post your first 10 videos before optimising anything — real data beats all theories
Video titles are your primary SEO lever — spend as much time on titles as on the concept
YouTube rewards watch-time percentage — 10-min video fully watched beats 20-min video half-watched
Shorts are a discovery tool, not a monetisation tool — use them to drive subscribers to long-form
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Starting with a channel trailer — algorithm doesn't recommend trailers; publish a real searchable video
Optimising thumbnails before 1K subscribers — A/B testing isn't available until then
Inconsistent upload schedule in months 1–3 — consistency beats frequency
Creating only trending content — evergreen tutorials get found for years; trending content has a 2-week shelf life
❓ FAQ
  • How long to reach YouTube monetisation?
    Average for a focused channel: 12–18 months. Main variables: upload consistency, niche size, and SEO quality. Some focused channels do it in 3–6 months.
  • Shorts or long-form first?
    Long-form builds watch time (required for monetisation). Shorts drive rapid subscriber discovery. Create long-form first, then clip 30–60 second highlights into Shorts.
  • How many subscribers to make money?
    YouTube Partner Programme: 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours. Beyond AdSense: sponsorships typically start at 5,000–10,000 subscribers; affiliate marketing from day one.
  • Best model for channel strategy?
    Claude is stronger for channel strategy — maintains coherent long-term planning and realistic milestone targets. ChatGPT is useful for quickly generating many title options.
