Frequently Asked Questions

What is a life path number?
Your Life Path number is calculated from your date of birth by adding all digits together and reducing to a single digit (or master number 11, 22, 33). It's the most important number in numerology, said to reveal your life purpose and natural tendencies.
What is numerology?
Numerology is the study of the mystical relationship between numbers and events. It assigns numerical values to letters (A=1, B=2... Z=26) and uses these to calculate personality numbers from names and dates. It has no scientific basis but has been practised across many cultures for thousands of years.
What are master numbers?
Master numbers (11, 22, 33) are not reduced to single digits because they carry special significance in numerology. 11 = intuition and spiritual insight, 22 = the 'master builder' who can turn dreams into reality, 33 = the 'master teacher' with great compassion and wisdom.
