📋 Prompt
You are a Shopify CRO specialist who has improved product page CVR from 2% to 6%+.

Product: [product name and description]
Current CVR: [current conversion rate or unknown]
Traffic source: [paid/organic/social]
Customer: [target customer]
Top objection: [price/trust/size/quality]

Task:
1. ABOVE-THE-FOLD: Title formula (include key spec), price presentation, CTA text/colour/placement, trust signals
2. PRODUCT DESCRIPTION: Hook (first 50 words), 5 Feature → Benefit bullets
3. SOCIAL PROOF: Review display, which snippets to feature, UGC integration
4. OBJECTION HANDLER: Specific section addressing [top objection]
5. SEO: Title tag (60 chars), meta description (155 chars), alt text formula
6. MOBILE: 3 Shopify-specific mobile CRO improvements
7. A/B TESTS: Top 3 in priority order
📄 Example output
SHOPIFY OPTIMISATION: Bamboo Desk Organiser → target 4%+ CVR

TITLE: 'Natural Bamboo Desk Organiser — 5 Compartments (20×12×10cm)'
Why: Dimensions in title removes the size objection before it forms.

CTA: 'Add to Bag' (outperforms 'Add to Cart' 7–12% in UK/EU tests)
TRUST (below CTA): ★★★★☆ 4.8/5 (234 reviews) | ✓ Free Returns | ✓ FSC Certified | ✓ Ships 24h

HOOK: 'Your desk should help you focus — not add to the chaos. The ToolsNova Bamboo Organiser holds pens, scissors, phone, and cables in 5 compartments. No assembly. Fits IKEA to standing desks.'

SIZE HANDLER:
• Slot 1 (8cm): Rulers, scissors | Slot 2 (4cm): Pens (up to 15) | Slot 3 (6cm): Phone upright | Slot 4 (5cm): Sticky notes | Slot 5 (3cm): USB cables

SEO TITLE: 'Bamboo Desk Organiser — 5 Compartments | ToolsNova' [51 chars]
META: 'Natural bamboo desk organiser with 5 compartments for pens, scissors and phone. FSC certified, free returns. 20×12×10cm fits all desks.' [135 chars]

TOP 3 A/B TESTS:
1. CTA text: 'Add to Bag' vs 'Get My Organiser'
2. Title: with vs without dimensions
3. Hero: product alone vs product in lifestyle desk setting

💡 Pro Tips
First product image does more work than any other element — it must be a lifestyle shot in use
Dimensions are the most common reason for abandonment — make them impossible to miss
Reviews below 4.2 stars hurt more than no reviews — fix the product before displaying the rating
Free shipping threshold displayed on the page significantly increases average order value
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Long descriptions with no scannable structure — use bullets
Generic title without key specifications
No size guide for products involving fit or dimensions
No urgency element — 'In stock' is not urgency; '14 left' is
❓ FAQ
  • Good Shopify CVR?
    Cold paid traffic: 1–3% typical, 4%+ excellent. Warm organic: 3–5% typical, 7%+ excellent. Traffic source quality matters as much as the page.
  • How many product images?
    Minimum 5: white background, lifestyle/in-use, detail close-up, size context, UGC. Video adds 25–35% purchase confidence where motion or scale matters.
  • Best review apps?
    Judge.me (free) or Okendo. For upsell: Frequently Bought Together. For urgency: stock countdown. Don't install too many — each adds page load time.
  • ChatGPT or Claude?
    ChatGPT for punchy energetic lifestyle copy. Claude for technical products needing precise feature-benefit translation. Test both for your category.
