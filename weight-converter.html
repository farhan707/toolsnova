Frequently Asked Questions

How many pounds in a kilogram?
1 kilogram = 2.20462 pounds. Conversely, 1 pound = 0.453592 kg. Quick estimate: multiply kg by 2.2 to get pounds.
How do I convert kg to stones and pounds?
1 stone = 14 pounds = 6.35029 kg. To convert kg: divide by 6.35029 to get stones, then multiply the decimal part by 14 for extra pounds. Example: 75 kg = 11.81 stone = 11 stone 11.4 lbs.
What is the difference between mass and weight?
Mass is the amount of matter in an object (measured in kg). Weight is the force of gravity on that mass (measured in Newtons). In everyday usage, weight and mass are used interchangeably, but scientifically 1 kg of mass weighs about 9.81 Newtons on Earth.
How many grams in an ounce?
1 ounce (oz) = 28.3495 grams. 1 pound = 16 ounces = 453.592 grams. In cooking, recipes often use both metric (grams) and imperial (ounces) measurements.
