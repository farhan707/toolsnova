📋 Prompt
You are a newsletter growth consultant who has helped 50+ creators grow from 0 to 10000+ subscribers.

Topic: [what your newsletter is about]
Target subscriber: [who you write for]
Current subscribers: [count or starting from zero]
Cadence: [weekly/bi-weekly/monthly]
Monetisation goal: [paid newsletter/sponsorships/product sales/consulting/none yet]
Time available: [hours per week]

Task:
1. POSITIONING STATEMENT: Why this newsletter specifically — for whom, with what unique angle
2. CONTENT FRAMEWORK: 3-4 recurring content types that make each issue predictable and valuable
3. LIST BUILDING TACTICS (5): Specific for your topic — tactic, time per week, expected subscriber gain rate
4. LEAD MAGNET: One high-value free resource that converts visitors to subscribers
5. REFERRAL MECHANISM: How to make current subscribers grow your list for you
6. MONETISATION PATH: Realistic timeline and approach for your specific goal
7. 90-DAY SUBSCRIBER MILESTONE PLAN
📄 Example output
NEWSLETTER STRATEGY: AI Marketing Weekly

POSITIONING:
The weekly newsletter for marketing professionals who use AI tools — not theory about AI, but the specific prompts, workflows, and tools practitioners are actually using this week. Every issue: one tested prompt you can use before the next one arrives.

CONTENT FRAMEWORK:
1. PROMPT OF THE WEEK: One specific template, tested, with before and after examples
2. TOOL SPOTLIGHT: One AI tool evaluated honestly — what it is good for and what it is not
3. WORKFLOW BREAKDOWN: How a real marketer accomplished a specific task with AI
4. QUICK TAKE: One observation about the AI marketing landscape in 100 words

LIST BUILDING TACTICS:
1. Twitter threads (2hrs/week): Turn each issue into a thread. Final tweet: Full issue plus the prompt template — [subscribe link]. Expected: 10-30 subscribers per week from good threads.
2. LinkedIn carousel (2hrs/week): Adapt the workflow breakdown. CTA: Weekly prompt templates to your inbox. Expected: 5-15 per week.
3. Substack Recommendations: Recommend 5 complementary newsletters — they are likely to reciprocate. Expected: 50-200 per mutual recommendation.
4. Guest appearances (1hr/week research): Reach audiences already interested in AI and marketing.
5. Community participation: Genuinely useful presence in 2-3 communities where your audience lives.

LEAD MAGNET:
The AI Marketing Prompt Pack — 20 tested templates for the 5 most common marketing tasks. Instant download on subscribe.

90-DAY PLAN:
Day 30: 200 subscribers — Twitter/LinkedIn distribution and initial promotions
Day 60: 500 — first referral loop active, Substack recommendations starting
Day 90: 1000 — first sponsorship conversation possible at this size

💡 Pro Tips
Lead magnet should be immediately useful — something usable in 30 minutes converts better than a 50-page guide they will read later
Consistency builds trust more than quality — a reliably good newsletter beats an occasionally excellent one that sometimes skips
Referral mechanisms work only after 200+ engaged subscribers — build the list first, then activate referrals
Your first 100 subscribers come from your personal network; do not measure success by subscriber count in week 1
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Launching without a lead magnet — conversion rate from subscribe for the newsletter vs get this free resource is dramatically different
Treating each issue as separate — recurring elements that subscribers look forward to is a feature not a formula
Growing list without nurturing — 1000 engaged subscribers converts better than 5000 passive ones
Starting paid too early — first 500 subscribers should get exceptional free content
❓ FAQ
  • When to launch paid tier?
    When: 500+ free subscribers, open rates above 40%, readers asking to pay for more. Your paid tier must offer meaningfully more than the free tier.
  • What is a good open rate?
    40-50% is excellent for a niche newsletter. 25-35% is healthy. Under 20% suggests engagement issues — audit your subject lines, sending frequency, and content match.
  • Substack vs ConvertKit vs Beehiiv?
    Substack: easiest start, best for paid subscriptions, built-in discovery. ConvertKit: best automation for product creators. Beehiiv: best monetisation features with built-in ads and referral programme.
  • Best model?
    Claude builds more strategically coherent newsletter positioning and realistic growth timelines. ChatGPT is faster for generating many content framework variations.
