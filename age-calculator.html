Frequently Asked Questions

How is exact age calculated?
The calculator subtracts your date of birth from today's date, accounting for varying month lengths and leap years, giving years, months, and days.
What is a leap year?
A leap year has 366 days (February 29). If born on Feb 29, most systems count the birthday on March 1 in non-leap years. The total days count includes all leap years since birth.
