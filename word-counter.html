Frequently Asked Questions

How does the word counter count words?
Words are split by whitespace (spaces, tabs, newlines). Any sequence of non-whitespace characters counts as one word — matching how most editors and publishing platforms count.
How is reading time calculated?
Reading time uses the average adult reading speed of 238 words per minute (wpm), based on multiple academic studies.
What are stop words in the frequency chart?
Stop words are extremely common words (the, a, and, in, is) filtered from the frequency analysis because they appear in almost every text and don't reveal meaningful writing patterns.
What character count does SEO require?
Google meta descriptions: 150–160 characters. Twitter: 280. Instagram captions: 2,200. LinkedIn posts: 3,000 characters.
