Niche: Productivity / Tech / Marketing creators | Budget: $300–$1,000 per post

💡 Pro Tips
Reference one specific piece of their content in the pitch — it's proof you've watched their work and instantly differentiates you from mass-outreach
The best influencer partnerships are with creators who'd recommend your product without payment — find those people first
Micro-influencers (10K–100K) typically have 3–5x better ROI than mega-influencers for product awareness campaigns
Always provide a product first, brief second — creators can't pitch what they haven't tried
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Sending the same pitch to 200 creators without personalisation — 0.5% response rate vs. 20%+ for personalised outreach
Micro-managing the creative — creators know their audience better than you do; give them the facts and let them tell the story
Not including FTC disclosure requirements in the brief — this is your legal responsibility as the brand
Paying only in product — creators with meaningful audiences have bills; product-only offers signal you don't value their work
❓ FAQ
  • What's a good engagement rate for an influencer?
    Instagram: 3–6% is good. Under 1% suggests bought followers or disengaged audience. TikTok: 5–10% is average (TikTok inherently has higher engagement). YouTube: 4–8% on video comments relative to views. Engagement rate is far more important than follower count.
  • How do I track ROI from influencer campaigns?
    Give each creator a unique discount code or UTM-tracked link. Track sales, trial sign-ups, or traffic from each creator. Compare cost-per-acquisition to your other channels. For brand awareness campaigns, track mention volume, search traffic spikes, and follower growth.
  • Should I work with a small number of large creators or many micro-influencers?
    For product launches: a mix of 2–3 mid-size creators and 10–20 micro-influencers often outperforms either alone. Micro-influencers have higher trust and conversion rates; larger creators provide reach and credibility. Budget allocation: 50/50.
  • Can I use AI to research influencers?
    AI can help you draft outreach and briefs, but influencer discovery requires real tools: Social Blade (free, audience stats), HypeAuditor (paid, fake follower detection), or Creator.co. For smaller budgets, manual research on the platform itself is more reliable than generic AI suggestions.
