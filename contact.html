Frequently asked questions

Is ToolsNova really free?

Yes. All 8 tools are completely free with no usage limits, no account required, and no paywall. The site is supported by non-intrusive advertising.

Does my data get sent to a server?

No. Every tool runs entirely in your browser using standard JavaScript APIs. Nothing you type, paste, or upload is transmitted to any server. We do not log inputs.

Can I suggest a new tool?

Absolutely — that's one of the best reasons to contact us. Use the form above and select "Tool suggestion." The most-requested tools get built first.

Can I use ToolsNova offline?

After your first visit, the browser caches the CSS and JavaScript files. The tool pages themselves will work offline as long as you've visited them before.
