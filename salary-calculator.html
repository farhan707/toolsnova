Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert hourly to annual salary?
Multiply your hourly rate by the number of working hours per year. Assuming 40 hours/week × 52 weeks = 2,080 working hours. So $25/hour × 2,080 = $52,000/year.
How do I calculate monthly salary from annual?
Divide annual salary by 12. Example: $60,000/year ÷ 12 = $5,000/month. For weekly: divide by 52. For daily: divide by 260 (52 weeks × 5 working days).
What is CTC vs take-home salary?
CTC (Cost to Company) includes everything the employer pays: base salary, allowances, employer's PF contribution, gratuity, and benefits. Take-home (in-hand) salary is what you actually receive after deducting income tax, PF, and other deductions — typically 70–80% of CTC.
How is income tax calculated on salary?
Income tax is calculated on taxable income (salary minus exemptions and deductions). Tax slabs vary by country. In India, the new regime starts taxing at 5% above ₹3 lakh. The tax estimate shown here is illustrative — consult a CA for accurate calculations.
