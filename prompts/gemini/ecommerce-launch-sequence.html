📋 Prompt
You are an e-commerce launch strategist who has generated £500K+ in first-week revenue for product launches.

Product: [product name and description]
Platform: [Shopify/WooCommerce/Amazon/own site]
Email list: [subscriber count or 'none yet']
Social following: [following or 'none yet']
Launch date: [date or 'in X weeks']

Task:

PRE-LAUNCH (2 weeks before):
3 teaser email subjects + preview text | Social posts for 7 days | Waitlist setup

LAUNCH DAY:
Email 1 (7am): Complete launch announcement
Email 2 (12pm): Social proof / momentum
Email 3 (8pm): Last-chance for launch offer
4 social posts with copy

POST-LAUNCH (days 2-7):
Day 2: Win story email | Day 4: FAQ/objection email | Day 7: Final call

ABANDONED CART:
Email 1 (1hr): Complete copy
Email 2 (24hr): Objection handling
Email 3 (72hr): Final offer

METRICS: What to track after day 7
📄 Example output
LAUNCH: Bamboo Desk Organiser

PRE-LAUNCH EMAIL 1 (Day -14):
Subject: Something new is coming to your desk 🪵
Preview: We've been building this for 6 months. Here's your first look.
'It started with a cluttered desk and a question: why is everything on a desk made of plastic? On [date], we're launching something we're genuinely proud of. You'll be first to know.'

LAUNCH DAY EMAIL 1 (7am):
Subject: It's here — The ToolsNova Bamboo Organiser
Preview: 5 compartments, solid bamboo, yours now →
'Today's the day. The Bamboo Desk Organiser is live.
✓ FSC-certified bamboo | ✓ 5 compartments | ✓ 20×12×10cm | ✓ Free delivery over £40
We made 200 units. When they're gone, restock is 8 weeks.
[Shop Now →]'

ABANDONED CART EMAIL 1 (1 hour):
Subject: You left something on your desk 🪵
'Hi [Name], Your Bamboo Organiser is waiting. We only made 200 for this first run.
[Complete your order →]'

DAY 7 METRICS:
Email open rate (35%+) | Click-through (3-5%) | Email-to-purchase (1-3%) | Cart recovery (5-8%)

💡 Pro Tips
Pre-launch waitlist converts at 3-5× your general list — highest-leverage email you'll send
Launch email at 7-8am in your audience's timezone — not 9am when they're already in meetings
Abandoned cart sequence recovers 5-15% of lost revenue — set it up before launch day
Day 2 '47 people bought in first 12 hours' dramatically increases urgency for those who didn't buy day 1
⚠️ Common Mistakes
No pre-launch sequence — cold launching produces 50% lower conversion
No waitlist — 'first access' framing creates urgency and builds your list
Abandoned cart with no objection handling — generic 'you left something' converts poorly
Sending all 3 launch-day emails regardless of sales velocity — adjust messaging based on what's happening
❓ FAQ
  • How many pre-launch emails?
    3-5 over 2 weeks. Too many exhaust the list before launch day.
  • Launch discount or launch bonus?
    Discount trains customers to wait for sales. Bonus (free shipping, gift) creates urgency without reducing the price.
  • How long should the launch offer run?
    7 days maximum with a clear defined end. Unlimited availability kills urgency.
  • Best model?
    ChatGPT for punchy energetic launch copy. Claude for higher price points where trust-building matters more.
