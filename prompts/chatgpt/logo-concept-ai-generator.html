📋 Prompt
You are a brand identity designer who has created visual identities for 200+ companies.

Business name: [name]
Industry: [what the business does]
Target audience: [primary customer]
Brand personality (3 words): [e.g. trustworthy, modern, bold]
Style preference: [minimal/geometric/wordmark/icon plus text/emblem/abstract]
AI tool: [Midjourney/DALL-E 3/other]

Task:
1. BRAND POSITIONING: One sentence capturing what the brand should feel like
2. LOGO CONCEPTS (3 directions): Each with rationale, AI image prompt, colour palette with hex codes, typography direction
3. COLOUR PALETTE: Primary, secondary, accent with hex codes and emotional associations
4. TYPOGRAPHY: Heading style and body style with rationale
5. LOGO USAGE RULES: Where each version works — full colour, one-colour, reversed
6. WHAT TO AVOID: 3 visual directions sending the wrong message
7. NEXT STEPS: What a designer needs to refine from AI output to final asset
📄 Example output
BRAND POSITIONING:
ToolsNova should feel like the smartest tool in the room that does not need to tell you it is smart.

CONCEPT 1 — THE SPARK:
Rationale: Geometric spark suggesting speed and insight without cliche
Prompt: /imagine ToolsNova minimal logo, abstract geometric spark icon, clean sans-serif wordmark, charcoal and electric blue, negative space design, vector flat style, white background, professional tech brand --ar 3:1 --v 6.1 --style raw
Palette: Charcoal #1a1a2e, Electric Blue #0066FF, White #ffffff

CONCEPT 2 — THE HEXAGON:
Rationale: Interconnected hexagons suggesting tools, structure, and precision
Prompt: /imagine ToolsNova logo, minimal hexagon grid icon, two hexagons intersecting, dark navy and gold, clean geometric wordmark, modern tech logo, vector flat, white background --ar 3:1 --v 6.1 --style raw
Palette: Navy #0d1b2a, Gold #f4a261, Light Grey #f8f8f8

COLOUR PALETTE (Concept 1):
#1a1a2e Charcoal — authority, depth, sophistication
#0066FF Electric Blue — intelligence, energy, digital
#ffffff White — clarity, space, cleanliness

TYPOGRAPHY:
Headings: Inter Bold or Syne — geometric, modern, highly legible on screens
Body: Inter Regular — maximum readability across all screen sizes
Avoid: serif fonts which read as traditional rather than tech-forward

AVOID:
1. Comic or bubbly fonts — undermines the reliable personality
2. Stock lightning bolt or cog icons — overused in tech branding
3. More than 3 colours in the primary logo — visual noise at small sizes

💡 Pro Tips
Test your logo at 16x16 pixels (favicon size) — if the icon is readable, it is strong enough
Logo must work in one colour only — if it needs multiple colours to be understood, the concept is too complex
Avoid gradients in the primary logo — they do not print well and date quickly
AI logo output is always a starting point — needs a designer for vector conversion, refinement, and proper file formats
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Choosing a concept that looks good large but breaks at small sizes — always test the smallest use case
Picking a trending style rather than timeless — logos need to work for 5-10 years
Too literal — a bakery logo does not need bread in it
Going straight to prompts without brand positioning — without it all concepts look generic
❓ FAQ
  • Can I trademark an AI-generated logo?
    This is evolving and jurisdiction-dependent. Purely AI-generated images have faced trademark challenges in the US. Most brands use AI for concept generation and have a designer create the final asset. Consult a trademark attorney for your situation.
  • Midjourney vs DALL-E 3 for logos?
    Neither produces print-ready vector logos. Midjourney produces more aesthetically polished concepts. DALL-E 3 follows text specifications more literally. Both outputs need vector conversion before professional use.
  • How many logo concepts to explore?
    3 is the professional standard — enough variety to represent genuinely different directions without overwhelming decision-makers.
  • Best model?
    Claude produces more nuanced brand positioning and strategic rationale. ChatGPT is faster and generates more prompt variations quickly.
