📋 Prompt
You are a brand design director who creates logo concepts for AI image generators.

Brand: [brand name]
Industry: [industry]
Values: [brand values — 3 words]
Style: [style preference — minimal/geometric/typographic/illustrative/retro/tech]

Task: Write 5 logo concept prompts:

For each concept:
CONCEPT NAME: Describe the design direction
DESIGN DESCRIPTION: What the mark looks like
AI PROMPT: Optimised for Midjourney or DALL-E 3
COLOUR RECOMMENDATION: Palette with hex codes
WHERE THIS WORKS: Best applications (app icon/business card/website/merchandise)

Finish with: Which concept is most versatile + common logo generation mistakes with AI
📄 Example output
BRAND: ToolsNova | INDUSTRY: AI/Tech tools | VALUES: Practical, Specific, Free | STYLE: Minimal/Geometric

CONCEPT 1 — THE PRECISION MARK:
Design: A stylised 'T' and 'N' monogram formed from geometric shapes — specifically two interlocking right angles that suggest both tools and precision. Clean, minimal, single-colour capable.
AI Prompt: 'Minimal geometric logo for a tech tools company, monogram combining letters T and N using clean right-angle geometric shapes, flat design, professional, scalable, white background, single colour' --ar 1:1 --style raw --v 6.1
Colour: Primary #7fff6f (brand green), Secondary #0c0c0e (dark)
Works best: App icon, favicon, small applications

CONCEPT 2 — THE NOVA BURST:
Design: Abstract starburst/spark mark with precisely 8 rays of varying lengths — suggesting both a nova (star explosion) and a toolbox opening. Geometric, energetic, works in monochrome.
AI Prompt: 'Geometric logo mark, abstract starburst with 8 precise rays of varying lengths, clean vector style, minimal, tech brand, professional, white background, flat design, scalable icon'
Colour: #7fff6f on dark, or dark on light
Works best: Social media profile, large applications, merchandise

COMMON AI LOGO MISTAKES:
❌ Asking for text in the logo — AI cannot reliably generate specific text, especially fonts
❌ Too complex — AI logos need to work at 16px (favicon); complexity becomes mud
❌ Asymmetric random shapes — logos need visual balance and intentionality

MOST VERSATILE CONCEPT: Concept 1 — monogram marks work across all sizes and applications, from app icons to billboards, in any colour on any background

💡 Pro Tips
Never ask AI to include specific text in a logo — it generates unreliable letter forms. Add text separately in a design tool.
The simplest logos are the most versatile — if it doesn't work at 16×16px, it's too complex
Generate 20+ variations and shortlist 3–5 — AI logo generation requires iteration
Use AI outputs as starting concepts for human refinement in Illustrator or Figma — treat them as references, not finals
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Expecting final production-ready logos from AI — use AI for concept exploration, not final deliverables
Not testing the logo at small sizes — most AI-generated logos are too detailed for icons
Ignoring background versatility — a good logo works on white, dark, and coloured backgrounds
Choosing a logo based on how it looks at full size without testing at 32px and 512px
❓ FAQ
  • Can AI generate a professional final logo?
    AI can generate strong conceptual direction that a designer refines into a professional mark. For a final logo, always involve a graphic designer who can create proper vector files (SVG/EPS) and ensure the mark works across all required applications.
  • What makes a good logo?
    Simplicity (works at any size), memorability (distinctive silhouette), versatility (works in black and white), appropriateness (fits the industry and brand personality), and timelessness (not trend-dependent). The best logos are deceptively simple.
  • Midjourney vs. DALL-E 3 for logo concepts?
    Midjourney produces more aesthetically refined marks but is harder to control precisely. DALL-E 3 follows specific instructions more literally. For logo exploration, try both — Midjourney for creative direction, DALL-E 3 for specific structural requirements.
  • How much does a professional logo design cost?
    Freelance logo design: $200–$2,000 for quality work. Design agencies: $2,000–$20,000+. Platforms like 99designs or Fiverr: $50–$500 (quality varies widely). AI-assisted logo design (human designer using AI tools) is emerging as a cost-effective middle ground.
