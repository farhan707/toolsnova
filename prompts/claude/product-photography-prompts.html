📋 Prompt
You are a product photographer and creative director who has shot campaigns for D2C brands.

Product: [product name and description]
Brand feel: [luxury/minimal/playful/natural/bold/technical]
Target audience: [who buys this]
Platform: [Instagram/Amazon/Shopify/ads/all]
AI tool: [Midjourney/DALL-E 3/Stable Diffusion]

Generate 5 distinct product photography prompts:

1. HERO SHOT: Clean product-only on neutral background for the product page
2. LIFESTYLE SHOT: Product in use or in context for social media
3. FLATLAY: Product and complementary props from directly above for Instagram
4. DETAIL/TEXTURE: Close-up on a specific material or feature
5. MOOD/BRAND: Evokes brand feeling more than shows product — for ads

For each include:
- Full AI image prompt ready to copy and paste
- Camera angle and distance
- Lighting style
- Colour palette notes
- Recommended use case
📄 Example output
PRODUCT SHOTS: Bamboo Desk Organiser

1. HERO SHOT:
Prompt: /imagine bamboo desk organiser, minimalist product photography, pure white background, soft diffused studio lighting, slight shadow underneath, 45-degree front angle, sharp focus, no props, photorealistic commercial product shot --ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --style raw --q 2
Use: Main product image on Shopify and Amazon

2. LIFESTYLE SHOT:
Prompt: /imagine bamboo desk organiser on a clean oak desk, MacBook to the left, small succulent plant, warm morning window light, professional workspace, shallow depth of field, warm muted tones, lifestyle product photography --ar 4:5 --v 6.1 --stylize 200
Use: Instagram feed post, Pinterest

3. FLATLAY:
Prompt: /imagine bamboo desk organiser flatlay, directly overhead shot, white linen surface, stationery arranged around it, morning coffee cup corner, natural soft shadows, minimal styling, warm neutral palette --ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --stylize 150
Use: Instagram square, Stories

4. DETAIL SHOT:
Prompt: /imagine macro close-up bamboo wood grain texture, desk organiser corner detail, soft warm lighting, shallow depth of field, extremely sharp commercial product detail --ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --style raw
Use: Product page additional images, carousel ads

5. MOOD SHOT:
Prompt: /imagine calm productive home office, minimal desk setup, bamboo organiser visible, warm morning light, plants, aspirational lifestyle, bokeh background, warm golden tones --ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 300
Use: Facebook ads, website hero banner

💡 Pro Tips
Hero shot should be pure white or neutral — required for Amazon and performs best for product pages
Use --style raw in Midjourney for product photography — removes stylisation that makes commercial shots look illustrated
Lighting is the most important variable — soft diffused or window light avoids harsh shadows that cheapen products
Aspect ratio matters: 1:1 for Instagram feed, 4:5 for optimal reach, 16:9 for ads, 3:4 for Pinterest
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Too many props in lifestyle shots — one or two complementary items maximum
Not specifying the camera angle — product photography alone produces random compositions
Not saving prompts that work — document winning combinations as your brand house style prompt
Using hero shot prompts for ads — mood and lifestyle shots convert better for ad placements
❓ FAQ
  • Can AI product photography be used for real ecommerce listings?
    Yes — many brands use AI-generated imagery for lifestyle and mood shots. Hero shots on Amazon require real product photos. AI can also edit real photos to change backgrounds.
  • Midjourney vs DALL-E 3?
    Midjourney generally produces more photorealistic commercially polished imagery. DALL-E 3 is better at maintaining specific product details as described. Test both for your product.
  • How to maintain consistency across a product line?
    Use Midjourney's Style Reference (--sref) with a reference image, or keep a detailed house style prompt with consistent lighting, background, and aesthetic specifications applied to all products.
  • Best model?
    ChatGPT generates photography prompts quickly with good technical vocabulary. Claude produces more thoughtful rationale for each image type and brand direction.
