📋 Prompt
You are a professional AI art director specialising in product photography prompts for DALL-E 3.

Product: [product description]
Style: [photography style — studio/lifestyle/flat lay/editorial/minimalist]
Background: [background — white studio/marble/wood/lifestyle setting]
Use: [intended use — e-commerce hero/social media/ads/website]

Task: Write 4 DALL-E 3 product photography prompts:

For each variation (DALL-E 3 uses natural language):
- Full descriptive prompt in conversational English
- Lighting setup specification
- Camera/lens reference (improves realism in training)
- Composition and framing
- Mood
- Notes on which use case this serves best

VARIATION 1: Clean studio hero shot
VARIATION 2: Lifestyle context shot
VARIATION 3: Flat lay arrangement
VARIATION 4: Macro/detail shot

ALSO: DALL-E tips for product photography + common mistakes
📄 Example output
PRODUCT: Natural bamboo desk organiser

VARIATION 1 — STUDIO HERO (best for: e-commerce main image):
A professional product photography studio shot of a natural bamboo desk organiser containing pens, scissors, and a ruler. Clean white background, soft three-point studio lighting creating gentle shadows on the right side. Camera angle: 45 degrees from above-level, product fills 70% of frame. The bamboo grain is clearly visible with warm golden undertones. Shot on a Canon 5D Mark IV with 100mm macro lens at f/8 for full depth of field. Hyper-realistic product photography, slight drop shadow beneath product, no reflections on background.

VARIATION 2 — LIFESTYLE (best for: social media, About page):
Lifestyle photograph of a bamboo desk organiser on a light oak desk in a bright minimalist home office. Morning light from the left through sheer curtains, warm colour temperature. MacBook laptop, open notebook, and coffee mug in soft bokeh background (f/2.8). The organiser contains pens and a small succulent. 45-degree angle from above. Colour palette: warm whites, natural wood tones, soft greens. Photorealistic lifestyle photography.

VARIATION 3 — FLAT LAY (best for: Instagram, Pinterest):
Flat lay photography arrangement on white marble surface. Bamboo desk organiser centred with office supplies arranged around it: copper paperclips, leather notebook, dried botanicals, three pens, scissors. Bird's eye view, 90 degrees. Perfect symmetry, soft natural daylight, no harsh shadows. Clean editorial flat lay photography with breathing room between elements.

VARIATION 4 — DETAIL MACRO (best for: product detail images, texture emphasis):
Extreme close-up of bamboo desk organiser surface texture. Natural bamboo grain fills the entire frame. Macro lens, razor-sharp focus on where two bamboo strips meet. Dramatic side lighting creating micro-shadows in the grain texture. Deep warm browns and honey tones. Shot at f/16 for full macro depth-of-field. Pure texture study — no background visible.

💡 Pro Tips
Specify camera and lens details — these phrases are associated with professional photography in training data and dramatically improve output quality
'Photorealistic product photography' and 'professional studio' together improve realism vs. either phrase alone
Request 'no text, no watermarks, no labels' explicitly — DALL-E 3 sometimes adds these unprompted
Use Variation (stronger) in ChatGPT to iterate on promising images rather than regenerating from scratch
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Vague prompts like 'photo of product on white background' — the more specific, the more control you have
Not specifying lighting — lighting is 80% of what makes product photography look professional
Asking for too many objects in one frame — fewer objects = more accurate rendering of each
Forgetting 'no distortion' for products with text or logos — these often get rewritten by the model
❓ FAQ
  • DALL-E 3 vs. Midjourney for product photos?
    DALL-E 3 follows detailed natural language instructions more literally and is better controlled. Midjourney produces more aesthetically striking images but with less precise instruction-following. For exact product photography requirements, DALL-E 3 is more controllable.
  • Can I use DALL-E product photos commercially?
    OpenAI's 2026 policy allows commercial use of images generated via their API and ChatGPT Plus. Always check the current Terms of Service for your specific plan. Disclose AI generation where required by platform or jurisdiction.
  • How do I get consistent product photos across a catalogue?
    Create one 'master prompt' for your brand's visual style and modify only the product description for each new product. Consistent: background, lighting description, camera/lens, angle. Variable: product name and specific details.
  • Can Gemini generate product photos?
    Gemini Ultra includes Imagen 3 for image generation, which produces high-quality product photography. The prompt structure is similar — descriptive natural language. For e-commerce at scale, dedicated image generation APIs (Stability AI, Adobe Firefly) offer better consistency and IP protection.
