📋 Prompt
You are an Instagram content creator with 500K+ followers.

Topic: [reel topic]
Audience: [target audience]
Length: [15/30/60/90 seconds]
Goal: [followers/saves/comments/website traffic]

Task: Write a complete Instagram Reel script:

[0–3s] HOOK: Stops the scroll — must work without sound
[TEXT OVERLAY]: On-screen text for silent viewers

[3s to (length–5s)] VALUE: Fast, specific, no padding
Each point: Spoken line + [TEXT OVERLAY] + [VISUAL suggestion]

[Final 5s] CTA: One specific ask matching [goal]
[TEXT OVERLAY]: CTA visual

ALSO:
- Caption (first 125 visible chars)
- Hashtags: 5 niche + 3 medium + 2 broad
- Audio suggestion
- Cover image text
📄 Example output
REEL: '3 AI prompts every content creator should use' — 30s

[0–3s]
Say: 'If you use AI for content, you're missing the one prompt type that matters most.'
OVERLAY: 'AI prompts creators don't share 🤫'

[3–8s]
Say: 'The rewrite prompt — instead of writing from scratch: Rewrite this in my voice, keep these 3 ideas, cut the rest.'
OVERLAY: 'REWRITE PROMPT → paste your draft'

[8–16s]
Say: 'The angle finder — give AI your topic, ask for 10 angles, pick the best one.'
OVERLAY: 'ANGLE FINDER → 10 ideas in 30s'

[16–24s]
Say: 'The gap prompt — show AI the top 3 posts on your topic, ask what they all missed. That gap is your content.'
OVERLAY: 'GAP PROMPT → find what competitors missed'

[24–30s]
Say: 'Save this. Follow for one AI tip per day.'
OVERLAY: 'Save 🔖 + Follow for daily AI tips'

CAPTION: 'The AI prompts that improve content quality (not just speed) 👇 Save before you open ChatGPT next time'
HASHTAGS: #contentcreatortools #aicontent #chatgpttips #promptengineering #contentcreatortips + #aitools #contentcreator #marketingtips + #ai #socialmediatips
AUDIO: Trending upbeat lo-fi
COVER: '3 AI prompts creators hide'

💡 Pro Tips
Hook must work without audio — 60% of Reels watched silently; text overlay carries the hook
Saves are the highest-value metric on Instagram — create content people want to return to
Cover image is what non-followers see in your grid — communicate value in 4 words
Instagram algorithm rewards early saves/shares in the first 30 minutes most heavily
⚠️ Common Mistakes
No text overlay — silent viewers get no value and swipe away
Hook requiring context — first 3 seconds must work with zero prior knowledge of you
Weak CTA — 'like and comment' underperforms; 'save this' or 'send to a friend' dramatically outperforms
Ignoring the cover image — the static thumbnail is what subscribers see in your grid
❓ FAQ
  • Best Reel length?
    7–15 seconds for maximum reach. 30–60 seconds for value and saves. 90 seconds for educational deep-dives. Check your Instagram Insights — optimal length varies by niche.
  • How to make Reels go viral?
    Most reliable triggers: saves, shares to Stories, and comments asking questions. Create content that is immediately useful, surprising, or validates something viewers believe but haven't articulated.
  • Should I use trending audio?
    Trending audio gives a modest boost — don't contort your content to fit a trend. Use it when it fits naturally; prioritise content quality otherwise.
  • ChatGPT or Claude?
    ChatGPT produces more energetic, hook-forward scripts that match social media pacing. Claude is better for educational content requiring more careful structure and nuance.
