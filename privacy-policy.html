Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 January 2026  ·  Last updated: 1 January 2026

Plain-English summary: ToolsNova tools run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit the text, data, or files you use in any tool. The only data we receive is standard web server access logs and, if you have consented, anonymised analytics and advertising data from third-party services.

1. Who we are

ToolsNova ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at toolsnova.net (the "Site"). We provide free, browser-based developer utilities. For any privacy questions, you can reach us via our contact page or at privacy@toolsnova.net.

2. Information we do NOT collect

Because all tool functionality runs locally in your browser using JavaScript, we have no technical ability to see or store:

  • The JSON, text, or code you paste into any formatter or converter
  • Passwords generated by the Password Generator
  • Any text entered into the Word Counter, Case Converter, or Lorem Ipsum Generator
  • Any URLs or Base64 strings processed by the encoder/decoder tools
  • Any timestamps entered into the Timestamp Converter

This data never leaves your device. It is processed entirely by your browser's JavaScript engine.

3. Information we do collect

3a. Server access logs

When you visit any page on this Site, our web host automatically records standard access log data, including:

  • Your IP address (typically truncated or anonymised)
  • The page URL requested
  • The date and time of the request
  • Your browser type and operating system (user-agent string)
  • The referring URL (the page you came from, if any)

These logs are used solely for security monitoring and diagnosing technical issues. They are retained for a maximum of 30 days and are not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

3b. Analytics

We use anonymised, privacy-respecting web analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns — for example, which tools are most popular and how users navigate the Site. Analytics data does not include the content you enter into any tool. Where required by law, analytics tracking is only activated after you provide consent via our cookie notice.

3c. Advertising

This Site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense and similar third-party advertising networks. These services may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or aboutads.info.

We do not pass any tool input data to advertising networks.

3d. Contact form submissions

If you contact us via the contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message content. This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry and is not used for marketing or shared with third parties.

4. Cookies

ToolsNova itself does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party services (analytics, advertising) may set cookies subject to their own privacy policies. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent notice.

5. Third-party links and services

Our pages may link to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

6. Children's privacy

ToolsNova is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data retention

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months after the last correspondence. Server access logs are deleted after 30 days. We do not retain tool input data because we never receive it.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection law (such as the GDPR or CCPA) to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@toolsnova.net. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the limited personal information we do hold. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact us

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our contact page or email privacy@toolsnova.net.
