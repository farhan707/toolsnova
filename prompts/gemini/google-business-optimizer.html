📋 Prompt
You are a local SEO specialist who has increased Google Business Profile visibility by 300%+.

Business type: [business type]
Location: [city and area]
Top 3 services: [top 3 services]
Profile status: [new/existing with few reviews/established]

Task:
1. BUSINESS DESCRIPTION (750 chars max): Keyword-optimised, benefit-led, local references, main services natural
2. CATEGORIES: Primary + 5 secondary suggestions
3. REVIEW RESPONSES: 5-star (warm, specific, keyword-aware), 3-star (acknowledge + resolve), 1-star (professional, empathetic, offline resolution)
4. GOOGLE POSTS (weekly): 4 templates — Offer / Event / Product / Update
5. Q&A: 10 questions to pre-populate with keyword-rich answers
6. PHOTOS: Priority order of photos to upload
7. 7-DAY QUICK WINS: 5 specific actions this week
📄 Example output
OPTIMISATION: Specialty Coffee Shop, Shoreditch

DESCRIPTION (583 chars):
'Speciality coffee shop in Shoreditch serving single-origin espresso, filter coffee, and weekend brunch. Beans sourced directly from Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — roasted fresh weekly.

Coworking-friendly with fast WiFi, plenty of seating, and outlets at every table. Open from 7am weekdays. 5 minutes from Old Street station. Dog-friendly. Gluten-free and vegan options available.'

CATEGORIES: Primary: Coffee Shop
Secondary: Café, Breakfast Restaurant, Coworking Space, Brunch Restaurant, Specialty Coffee Shop

5-STAR RESPONSE:
'Thank you [Name]! So glad you enjoyed [specific mention]. Single-origin coffee and a welcoming Shoreditch space is exactly what we set out to create. See you soon! ☕'

1-STAR RESPONSE:
'Hi [Name], I'm really sorry your experience fell short. Please reach out at [email] so I can speak with you personally — I take every experience seriously and want to make this right.'

7-DAY QUICK WINS:
1. Add 10 photos: interior, coffee close-up, food, team, exterior
2. Pre-populate 10 Q&As (parking, WiFi, dogs, vegan, opening hours)
3. Save review response templates
4. Enable messaging
5. Post first weekly update

💡 Pro Tips
Your GBP category is the most important local SEO signal — check which categories top-ranking local competitors use
Responding to every review within 24 hours improves local ranking signals
Profiles with 100+ photos get 520% more calls than those with fewer than 10
Google Posts expire after 7 days — schedule recurring posts like social media
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Generic description with no local keywords — mention your neighbourhood and nearby landmarks
Ignoring negative reviews or responding defensively — every public response is read by prospective customers
No photos beyond a logo — Google Maps shows photos before the click
Not enabling messaging — Google rewards profiles where customers can contact directly
❓ FAQ
  • How does GBP affect local rankings?
    Google ranks local businesses in the map pack based on: relevance (matches search), distance (proximity), and prominence (reviews, links, completeness). Profile completeness and review count/rating are most controllable.
  • How to get more Google reviews?
    Ask in person at moment of highest satisfaction with a direct link. Never offer incentives — violates Google's guidelines.
  • How often to post?
    Minimum weekly. Posts expire after 7 days. Event and offer posts are weighted more than general updates.
  • Respond to old reviews?
    Yes — respond to all unresponded reviews, including old ones. A thoughtful response to an old negative review shows potential customers you take feedback seriously.
