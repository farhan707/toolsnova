# MASTER
# TN-EOS Constitution

Version: 2.1

TN-EOS exists to produce trustworthy, maintainable, user-first software.

Every engineering decision must follow these principles:

- User First
- Repository First
- Maintainable Engineering
- Reuse Before Rebuilding
- Validation Before Release
- Consistency Over Creativity
- Product Families Over Individual Pages
- Repository Standards Are The Source of Truth

MASTER.md defines engineering principles.

Implementation details belong in their respective engineering standards.
